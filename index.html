How to Vote

📅 Step 1: Mark Your Calendar

Elections are scheduled for 31 July 2026. Make sure you're on campus on election day!

📋 Step 2: Check Your Eligibility

Ensure you're a registered SLCMC student with a valid student ID. Visit the election office if you have questions.

🗳️ Step 3: Cast Your Vote

Come to the designated voting centers on campus. One student = One vote. Your vote matters!

✅ Step 4: Support HEAL Movement

Choose HEAL Movement candidates who are committed to your health, empowerment, advocacy, and leadership.

📍 Election Day: 31 July 2026
🕐 Voting Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the HEAL Movement? +
The HEAL Movement represents our commitment to Health, Empowerment, Advocacy, and Leadership. We are dedicated to serving the student community with integrity, transparency, and a genuine desire to make positive change.
When is the election? +
The SLCMC SRC elections are scheduled for 31 July 2026. Voting will take place from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM at designated polling stations on campus.
Who can vote? +
All registered SLCMC students with a valid student ID are eligible to vote. If you have questions about your eligibility, please contact the election office.
How do I support the HEAL Movement? +
You can support HEAL Movement by voting for our candidates, volunteering with the campaign, sharing our message with fellow students, and joining us in building a stronger community.
What are the main promises of HEAL Movement? +
Our main promises include improved student health services, better academic support, enhanced sports and entertainment programs, transparent governance, and genuine representation of student voices.
